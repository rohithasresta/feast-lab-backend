About University of Missouri

Dr. Krishnaswamy has joint appointment in the Dept of Chemical and Biomedical Engineering (COE) & the Division of Food, Nutrition and Exercise Sciences (CAFNR) at University of Missouri, Columbia. Interested students can apply to either COE or CAFNR.

Mizzou is the flagship campus of the University of Missouri system, established in 1839 as the first public university west of the Mississippi River — a top-tier Research 1 (R1) University and member of the Association of American Universities (AAU).
