Active Research Areas
From gut microbiome interactions to AI-powered dietary analysis, our work spans the full spectrum of nutritional science.

Diet & Metabolic Health

Longitudinal studies on how macronutrient composition affects insulin sensitivity, lipid profiles, and cardiometabolic risk.

Epidemiology
🦠

Gut Microbiome & Nutrition

How dietary fiber, fermented foods, and ultra-processed food consumption shape gut microbial communities and systemic inflammation.

Microbiome
🌍

Food Environment Mapping

GIS-based analysis of food access, food desert prevalence, and neighborhood food environments in Missouri.

AI-Powered Dietary Analysis
