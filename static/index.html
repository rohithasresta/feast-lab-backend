Food Engineering & Sustainable Technologies (FEAST) Lab

Welcome to the FEAST Lab! We are a transdisciplinary group of researchers working at the interface of science, engineering, and technology addressing Hidden Hunger (micronutrient deficiency). The FEAST Lab engineers solutions for a resilient food future — from studying underutilized ancient grains and agroforestry food systems to upcycling of food industry waste to recover micronutrients for developing smart delivery systems.

Our Mission

To advance breakthrough food and bioprocessing technologies that position food as medicine, strengthen food and nutrition security, and translate research innovations into real-world impact.
