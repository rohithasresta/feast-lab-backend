HOW TO JOIN A STUDY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
The FEAST Lab runs several studies that recruit community participants. Here's how to get involved:

STEP 1: Check eligibility
Each study has different requirements. General eligibility for most studies:
- Age 18–65
- No major metabolic conditions (unless specifically recruited for that population)
- Able to attend in-person visits at MU campus (some studies are remote-friendly)
- Not pregnant or planning pregnancy during study period

STEP 2: Express interest
Email feast-lab@missouri.edu with:
- Your name and age
- Which study you're interested in (or "any available study")
- Brief health background (any conditions, medications)
Our lab coordinator Sarah Hendricks will respond within 2 business days.

STEP 3: Screening call
A 15-minute phone screening to confirm eligibility and answer your questions.

STEP 4: Consent and enrollment
In-person or remote consent process, followed by baseline assessments.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: Is participation in studies safe?
A: All FEAST Lab studies are approved by the MU Institutional Review Board (IRB).
   Dietary intervention studies carry minimal risk. We do not conduct invasive procedures.
   Blood draws are performed by certified phlebotomists in our clinic.

Q: Do I need to change my diet to participate?
A: It depends on the study. Observational studies (like MOCS) ask you to maintain your
   normal diet. Intervention studies ask you to follow a specific dietary pattern for
   the study period.

Q: Are studies confidential?
A: Yes. All data is de-identified. Your name is never associated with your health data
   in any publication. We comply with HIPAA and all MU data security requirements.

Q: Can I leave a study after joining?
A: Yes. Participation is always voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without penalty.

Q: I'm not in Columbia — can I still participate?
A: Some studies are fully remote (online dietary recalls, app-based tracking). Others
   require at least one in-person visit. We'll let you know during screening.

Q: How do I get my results?
A: We share aggregate study results via our newsletter and website. Individual results
   (like your microbiome profile or metabolic markers) are shared at clinic visits.
